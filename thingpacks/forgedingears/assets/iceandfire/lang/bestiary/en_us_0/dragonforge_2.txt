To properly power a forge, one 
must place the chained or tamed 
dragon directly in front of the 
aperture. Then the dragon will 
begin to power the forge.
Most items, when placed in a forge,
will be turned to ash. But placing a 
steel ingot and dragon blood will 
result in a powerful alchemical metal.
